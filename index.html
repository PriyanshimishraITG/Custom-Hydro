How it Works

A gateway is required to connect the sensor network to the internet. It has Ethernet and Cellular capabilities.

The Gateway

+

"A gateway is required to connect the sensor network to the internet. It has Ethernet and Cellular capabilities. Collecting the data from up to 1,000 sensors and forwarding it to the SenseCAP browser based Portal dashboard and the SenseCRAFT moblie app."

SenseCRAFT mobile app

+

"Use the app to bind new devices to the network via bluetooth. Monitor sensor data by the day, week or month. View data graphs and historical measurements. Receive notifications on your mobile phone if a sensor measurement is out of parameters."

SenseCAP Sensors

+

"With a Sensecap Sensor Network, you can keep an eye on the temperature, humidity, CO2, soil moisture (volumetric water content), soil electrical conductivity (bulk EC and pore water EC) and other important factors in your greenhouse or on your farm, allowing you to optimize performance and ensure your plants stay healthy.."
